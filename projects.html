Projects

Here's some more information on one of my recent art projects.

Last fall I played in a Monster of the Week campaign. It was a ton of fun, and I really loved the character that I played. I had fun designing them, and their adventures really inspired my art. I drew them a lot, and this is just one of the pieces that I created based on them.

Their name is Riley, and they're a college student/barista/hardware supply store worker/monster hunter. They have a variety of hats, and part of their story was learning how to balance their mortal life with their supernatural experiences. The playbook for Riley was The Mundane, which is a playbook all about the "normal" person in a monster hunting gang, the person who has no special powers or connections to the unknown, who stumbled into this unnatural world and decided to stick around to help out.

Here you see my finalized piece, as well as a progress video for the creation of the piece. The art program I use, Procreate, allows you to export the progress videos of all of your works, which is super helpful, and the videos are always fun to watch.
